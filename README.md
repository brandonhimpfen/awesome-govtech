## Foundations & Concepts

- [GovTech Explained](https://www.worldbank.org/en/topic/governance/brief/govtech-putting-people-first) – Overview of government technology and public sector digital transformation.
- [Digital Government](https://www.oecd.org/gov/digital-government/) – OECD guidance on digital-by-default public services.
- [Public Sector Innovation](https://www.oecd.org/innovation/public-sector-innovation/) – Frameworks for innovation in government.
- [Once-Only Principle](https://joinup.ec.europa.eu/collection/once-only-principle) – Reducing administrative burden through data reuse.
- [Digital Public Infrastructure](https://www.undp.org/digital/digital-public-infrastructure) – Foundational digital systems for governments.

## Digital Government Platforms

- [GOV.UK Platform](https://www.gov.uk/service-manual) – UK government’s digital service design and delivery platform.
- [ServiceNow Government](https://www.servicenow.com/industries/public-sector.html) – Digital workflows for public sector operations.
- [Salesforce Government Cloud](https://www.salesforce.com/solutions/industries/government/) – CRM and case management for government agencies.
- [OpenGov](https://opengov.com/) – Cloud software for budgeting, performance, and transparency.
- [Tyler Technologies](https://www.tylertech.com/) – Integrated software solutions for local and state governments.

## Service Delivery & Case Management

- [Civica](https://www.civica.com/) – Software for public administration and citizen services.
- [Casebook](https://casebook.net/) – Case management platform for human services.
- [Bonterra Case Management](https://www.bonterratech.com/) – Case management for social services and nonprofits.
- [OpenCRVS](https://opencrvs.org/) – Open-source civil registration and vital statistics system.
- [ODK](https://getodk.org/) – Data collection platform for field-based government programs.

## Identity, Trust & Digital ID

- [Login.gov](https://login.gov/) – U.S. government single sign-on and identity verification.
- [GOV.UK One Login](https://www.sign-in.service.gov.uk/) – Unified identity platform for UK public services.
- [ID.me](https://www.id.me/) – Digital identity verification for government programs.
- [MOSIP](https://www.mosip.io/) – Open-source modular digital identity platform.
- [Verifiable Credentials](https://www.w3.org/TR/vc-data-model/) – W3C standard for decentralized digital identity.

## Data, Analytics & Open Data

- [CKAN](https://ckan.org/) – Open-source data portal platform for governments.
- [Socrata](https://socrata.com/) – Open data publishing and analytics platform.
- [Open Data Portal](https://opendatawatch.com/) – Resources for open government data initiatives.
- [Tableau Government](https://www.tableau.com/solutions/government) – BI and analytics for public sector data.
- [Power BI Government](https://powerbi.microsoft.com/government/) – Secure analytics for government agencies.

## Civic Engagement & Participation

- [Decidim](https://decidim.org/) – Open-source participatory democracy platform.
- [Consul](https://consulproject.org/) – Citizen participation platform for governments.
- [CitizenLab](https://www.citizenlab.co/) – Digital citizen engagement and consultation tools.
- [Pol.is](https://pol.is/) – AI-powered civic engagement and opinion mapping.
- [Bang the Table](https://www.bangthetable.com/) – Online community engagement platform.

## RegTech & Compliance

- [Ascent](https://www.ascentregtech.com/) – Regulatory intelligence and compliance automation.
- [ClauseMatch](https://www.clausematch.com/) – Policy and regulatory lifecycle management.
- [OpenRegs](https://openregs.com/) – Open regulatory data and analysis platform.
- [RegData](https://www.mercatus.org/regdata) – Quantitative dataset on regulatory restrictions.
- [Compliance.ai](https://www.compliance.ai/) – AI-driven regulatory change management.

## Procurement & Public Finance

- [Open Contracting Data Standard](https://standard.open-contracting.org/) – Global standard for transparent procurement data.
- [GovSpend](https://govspend.com/) – Government procurement and spending analytics.
- [Bonfire](https://www.gobonfire.com/) – Public sector procurement management software.
- [OpenGov Budgeting](https://opengov.com/products/budgeting-planning/) – Budgeting and planning tools for governments.
- [USASpending](https://www.usaspending.gov/) – U.S. federal spending transparency platform.

## Smart Cities & Urban Tech

- [Smart Cities Council](https://smartcitiescouncil.com/) – Resources for smart city development.
- [OpenStreetMap](https://www.openstreetmap.org/) – Open geospatial data for urban planning.
- [CitySDK](https://www.citysdk.eu/) – APIs for smart city services and data.
- [FIWARE](https://www.fiware.org/) – Open-source platform for smart city applications.
- [Urban Observatory](https://urbanobservatory.ac.uk/) – Urban data and analytics for policy and planning.

## AI & Automation in Government

- [OECD AI Policy Observatory](https://oecd.ai/) – AI governance and policy resources.
- [GovAI](https://www.gov.ai/) – Research on AI use in public administration.
- [Robotic Process Automation (RPA)](https://www.uipath.com/solutions/government) – Automating repetitive government workflows.
- [Policy Analytics](https://www.policyanalytics.co/) – Data science and AI for public policy analysis.
- [AI Register](https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/algorithmic-transparency-records) – Transparency records for government algorithms.

## Security, Privacy & Ethics

- [NIST Cybersecurity Framework](https://www.nist.gov/cyberframework) – Cybersecurity standards for public sector systems.
- [ISO/IEC 27001](https://www.iso.org/isoiec-27001-information-security.html) – Information security management standard.
- [GDPR](https://gdpr.eu/) – Data protection regulation affecting public sector services.
- [Privacy by Design](https://www.ipc.on.ca/privacy-by-design/) – Framework for embedding privacy in systems.
- [AI Ethics Guidelines](https://www.oecd.org/going-digital/ai/principles/) – OECD principles for trustworthy AI.

## Open Source for Government

- [18F](https://18f.gsa.gov/) – U.S. digital services consultancy for government agencies.
- [Digital Public Goods Alliance](https://digitalpublicgoods.net/) – Promoting open-source public digital infrastructure.
- [Open Source Observatory](https://joinup.ec.europa.eu/collection/open-source-observatory-osor) – Open-source adoption in government.
- [CivicTech.guide](https://civictech.guide/) – Directory of civic technology tools.
